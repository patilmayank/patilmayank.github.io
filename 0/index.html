Project 0: Camera Effects & Media

Part 1: The Wrong Way vs. The Right Way

Captured two photos of William at different zoom levels (1x and 5x) to demonstrate the effect of focal length on perspective and subject appearance.

Part 2: Architectural Perspective Compression

Used different focal lengths to capture the Haas building, showing how zooming in compresses perspective and alters the spatial relationship between objects.

Part 3: The Dolly Zoom

Created an animated GIF of stuffed animals using multiple frames to illustrate the dolly zoom effect, where the background appears to change size relative to the subject.
